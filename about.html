About

About My Logo

What does it mean?

Everyone has a symbol that represents them. This is mine.
It means my personality is a constantly evolving, which is why I'm best represented by a swirl with dashes. The swirl represents growth while trying to balance myself. I'm both confident and insecure, wild and tame, quiet and loud. Creative and analytical. Moreover, in terms of rebirth or growth, the spiral symbol represents the consciousness of beginning from the core or center and expanding outwardly. A brilliant shout out to the idea of life; while it may not go as planned, it still continues, hiccups and all. The dashes represent my ability to carry relationships throughout my life. I may be a little all over the place, but I'm a fiercly loyal and stable wife, friend, and employee, proudly dedicated to continuing my swirl.
